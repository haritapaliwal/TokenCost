## FAQ (5 Real Q&As)

**Q1: How does the audit work?**  
You enter your current AI subscriptions — which tools, which plans, how many seats, and your monthly spend. Our deterministic audit engine compares your setup against verified May 2026 pricing data and flags anywhere you're overpaying. No AI guesswork — it's pure pricing logic.

**Q2: Is this actually free?**  
Yes. No credit card, no account required. Fill in your tools, get your results, share the report. The only optional step is entering your email to save a permanent copy.

**Q3: How do you know the pricing is accurate?**  
We manually verify pricing for each tool against official pricing pages. The current data was last verified in May 2026. We note the verification date on every audit report.

**Q4: What tools do you cover?**  
Currently: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude (consumer + API), ChatGPT (consumer + OpenAI API), Gemini, and Windsurf. More tools added regularly.

**Q5: What does Credex get out of this?**  
If your audit reveals significant savings potential — especially on API usage — Credex may reach out about discounted AI credit bundles that could save you an additional 15–25%. We only contact high-savings cases, and only if you've provided your email.
